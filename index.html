Introduction

I am a niche internet microcelebrity on R/feedthememes who enjoys drinking liquid concrete.

About

I started playing Minecraft around 2016. I got into modded Minecraft maybe a year or two later. Mainly using the Technic launcher. Now I use Curseforge for everything. It was during my early days playing modded MC that I found a particularly nice mod by the name of “Immersive Engineering.” Since then, the immersive series (Immersive Engineering, Immersive Petroleum, and such) have been some of my favorite mods.

I am well known on r/feedthememes for drinking liquid concrete. The joke started because I thought it would be funny to make it so whenever anyone thought of Immersive Engineering, they would think of drinking liquid concrete (which isn’t even a feature in the mod).
